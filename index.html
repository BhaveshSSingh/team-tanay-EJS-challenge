What is this Challenge About?

This is a fun and intense challenge for completing the Book Eloquent JavaScript in 10 Days! and for Each Chapter of the Book you Have to make a Blog post about your understanding of the Chapter.

Details for the Challenge

  • 1.You should be a member of the #mastermind group on Discord to Participate in this Challenge

  • 2.You Have to write a Blog for each Chapter of the Book. The Book Has 21 Chapters so you should have 21 blog posts or more about each chapter. The Blog posts should be about your understanding of the chapter.

  • 3.The Challenge will be for 10 Days and only the first 5 people to complete the challenge will be selected.

  • 4.This challenge is for people who are looking to speed up their learning and have fun while doing so! if you think this is too much do not participate.It isn't necessary to participate in this challenge to get to level One
